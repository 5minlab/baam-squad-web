BAAM SQUAD

THE SWEETS FACTORY HAS BEEN TAKEN OVER BY ZOMBIES EATING HUMAN FLESH.. AND CANDIES!

Play as one of four brave agents to investigate the case and struggle for your life! Shoot, run and throw grenades in order to survive from infinite stream of zombies. You can play alone or co-op with up to three other players.

Features

  • Abandoned Sweets factory – Complete with automatic sliding doors, functioning traps and vending machines that sell a bunch of good buffing snacks.
  • Free Exploration – Use controller to walk or run without motion-sickness, teleportation and space restriction.
  • 5 difficulty levels; from easy to master.
  • Weapons – From classic pistol to leading-edge laser gun. Collect all +10 weapons and upgrade them to level 10.
  • Local/Online Co-op – Play with friends or other players around the world. Up to four players allowed.
  • Global leaderboard – Mark your name on the top by killing as many zombies as possible. Headshot is the key to get high score and saving bullets.
  • Realistic Hand Interaction – You can shoot guns, throw objects, push buttons and pull levers. Give hands to friends who are bleeding out.
